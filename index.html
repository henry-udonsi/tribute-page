Ode to a Lost One

Tribute to Chinyere Udonsi

The sweet breadth of life
Dances no longer, silence is her heart
Set free from her earth binds like a Lyfe
The one I love, from me departs

It was the immortal God
Called you from us did he
To sit by his side with thee
Irrespective of our sob

No goodbyes were said
Too soon you left
And so our tears we shed
For our loved one whose presence we are bereft

I shall never forget her smile
Or her face weary and kind
That is my beguile.
I too shall never forget in kind

Her cold and lifeless face
Her body in beauty adorn
As she lays limp in death`s own embrace
Her life from where it was torn.


"Unable are the loved to die, for love is immortality"

--- Emily Dickinson ---

To our mother, we say good bye.
To our grand ma, we say good bye.
To our aunt, we say good bye.
To our sister, we say good bye.

Adieu Chinyere Udonsi, may we meet again when our lives are done.
